We define an AI prioritization score based on weighted criteria:

Priority Score Formula:

Priority Score = V Ã— A Ã— F Ã— D C Ã— T

Where:

  • V = Business Value (Revenue growth, cost savings, market positioning)
  • A = Strategic Alignment (Vision alignment, competitive advantage)
  • F = Feasibility (Development complexity, integration readiness)
  • D = Data Readiness (Data availability, quality)
  • C = Cost & Risk (Financial investment, regulatory concerns)
  • T = Time-to-Value (Deployment speed, measurable impact timeframe)

Scoring Breakdown (1-5 Scale):

  • Higher scores indicate greater feasibility, alignment, and value.
  • Lower cost & time increase prioritization.
  • Dividing by Cost & Time favors high-impact, low-effort AI initiatives.
